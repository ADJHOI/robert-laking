+About

Although his wider interests are expansive and expanding, Robert’s practice is primarily text-based. Because the communication of ideas through the use of words is already a ubiquitous pursuit, he is interested in finding novel adaptations of various strategies which exist. This provides opportunities to explore the conventions of conventional media. Robert makes playful use of these, ranging from the grammatical, lexical, and conceptual, to the physical.
